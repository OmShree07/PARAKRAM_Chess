Rulebook for PARAKRAM'24 Chess Tournament

CHESS SPORTS CLUB JUIT

विद्या तत्व ज्योतिसम्म

System of Play

The tournament will be conducted in accordance with FIDE laws of chess.

This tournament will be team-based, with each university or college permitted to enter one team only.

Each team will consist of five players: four active players and one substitute.

Time Control (Blitz)

3 min + 2 second increment per move

Time Control (Rapid)

10 min + 5 second increment per move

Tie Break

In case of a tie, the latest FIDE rules shall come into effect.

Rules for Illegal Moves

(Rapid)

  • For the first two illegal moves by a player, the arbiter shall give two minutes of extra time to their opponent in each instance.
  • If the same player makes a third illegal move, the arbiter shall declare the game lost by that player.

(Blitz)

  • The arbiter shall give a warning to a player for the first illegal move in the first instance.
  • If the same player makes a second illegal move, the arbiter shall declare the game lost by that player.

Note

  • All players can participate in both Rapid and Blitz matches.
  • FIDE rules and regulations will be followed strictly.
  • Matches in Rapid will be conducted following a pool system.
  • Matches in Blitz will be knockouts.
